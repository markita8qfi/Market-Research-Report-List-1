<p><strong>The Automotive Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive ignition coil market is segmented into single-spark and multi-spark types. Single-spark ignition coils, typically used in conventional systems, generate a single high voltage pulse per ignition cycle, providing adequate combustion for most gasoline engines. In contrast, multi-spark ignition coils deliver multiple voltage pulses within the same cycle, enhancing fuel combustion efficiency and performance, especially in high-performance or modified vehicles. This differentiation addresses the varying needs of automotive manufacturers and fuel efficiency standards in the market.</p></p>
