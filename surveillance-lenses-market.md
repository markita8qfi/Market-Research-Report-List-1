<p><p>The surveillance lenses market consists of two main types: auto iris and manual iris lenses. Auto iris lenses automatically adjust their aperture based on changing light conditions, enabling optimal image quality without manual intervention, making them ideal for dynamic environments. In contrast, manual iris lenses require the user to adjust the aperture manually, providing more control over depth of field and exposure, but needing more attention in fluctuating light situations. Each type caters to different surveillance needs and preferences.</p></p>
