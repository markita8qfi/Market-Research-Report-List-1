<p><p>The 3D ultrasound device market is primarily categorized into two types: color ultrasound and black-and-white (B/W) ultrasound. Color ultrasound utilizes Doppler technology to visualize blood flow and provide detailed images with color overlays, enhancing diagnostic capabilities in vascular and cardiac applications. B/W ultrasound, on the other hand, focuses on capturing high-resolution grayscale images for reproductive health and anatomical assessments. Both types are essential in diagnostic imaging, offering distinct advantages based on clinical needs and applications.</p></p>
