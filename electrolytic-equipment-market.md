<p><p>The electrolytic equipment market encompasses three primary types based on solution pH levels: Acidity Electrolytic, Alkaline Electrolytic, and Neutral Electrolytic. Acidity Electrolytic equipment operates in acidic environments, facilitating processes like electroplating and metal refining. Alkaline Electrolytic equipment functions in alkaline conditions, suitable for applications like water electrolysis for hydrogen production. Neutral Electrolytic equipment, on the other hand, works within a neutral pH range, often used in processes such as desalination and wastewater treatment, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
