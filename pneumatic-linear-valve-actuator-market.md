<p><p>The pneumatic linear valve actuator market consists of three main types. Double-acting pneumatic linear valve actuators use air pressure to move the valve in both directions, providing greater control and efficiency. Single-acting pneumatic linear valve actuators rely on air pressure for movement in one direction, using a spring to return to the original position, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Other types may include specialized actuators designed for specific industrial applications, further diversifying market offerings.</p></p>
