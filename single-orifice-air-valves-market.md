<p><p>The Single Orifice Air Valves market is categorized into two primary types: flanged and threaded. Flanged air valves feature flat surfaces with holes for bolts, enabling secure attachment to pipelines, making them suitable for larger systems. Threaded air valves, on the other hand, use screw connections for easy installation and maintenance, ideal for smaller or less complex systems. Both types facilitate efficient air release and prevent vacuum formation in pipelines, optimizing fluid flow and enhancing system performance.</p></p>
