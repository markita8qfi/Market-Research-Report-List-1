<p><strong>The Rubber Repair Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Bond</li><li>Cold Bond</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber repair adhesive market is primarily categorized into hot bond and cold bond types. Hot bond adhesives are activated with heat, providing a strong and durable bond suitable for high-stress applications. They cure quickly and are ideal for repairs needing immediate durability. In contrast, cold bond adhesives do not require heat for activation, making them user-friendly and suitable for repairs that can cure at ambient temperatures. Both types are essential for various applications, including automotive, industrial, and consumer products.</p></p>
