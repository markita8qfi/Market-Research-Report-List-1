<p><strong>The Tire Retreading Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Cure</li><li>Mold Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tire retreading market is primarily divided into two types: pre cure and mold cure. Pre cure involves applying a pre-cured tread to a prepared tire casing, offering flexibility and efficiency, as the tread is ready-made and can be easily customized. Mold cure, on the other hand, involves placing a raw tread into a mold and curing it directly on the tire, resulting in a more integrated and durable finish. Both methods aim to extend tire lifespan and reduce waste, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
