<p><strong>The Tire Pressure Monitor Control Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wheel-Speed Based(Indirect TPMS)</li><li>Pressure-Sensor Based(Direct TPMS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tire Pressure Monitor Control Modules market is segmented into two primary types: Wheel-Speed Based (Indirect TPMS) and Pressure-Sensor Based (Direct TPMS). Indirect TPMS utilizes wheel speed sensors to estimate tire pressure by comparing rotational speeds, thus inferring pressure changes indirectly. In contrast, Direct TPMS employs pressure sensors mounted within the tire to provide real-time, accurate tire pressure readings. Both systems enhance vehicle safety and efficiency, catering to different automotive needs and technological advancements within the industry.</p></p>
