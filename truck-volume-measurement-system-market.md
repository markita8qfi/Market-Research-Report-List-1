<p><ul><li>Static Measurement System</li><li>Dynamic Measurement System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Truck volume measurement systems are categorized into two main types: static and dynamic measurement systems. Static measurement systems involve fixed installations where trucks are measured when they are stationary, providing accurate volume data based on specific dimensions. In contrast, dynamic measurement systems assess truck volume while in motion, using advanced technologies like sensors and cameras to capture real-time data. Both systems serve industries by facilitating efficient logistics, optimizing cargo loads, and ensuring compliance with transportation regulations.</p></p>
