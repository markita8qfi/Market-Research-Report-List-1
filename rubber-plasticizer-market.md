<p><p>The rubber plasticizer market comprises several types, each contributing to different properties in rubber formulations. Aromatic rubber plasticizers are derived from aromatic compounds, enhancing flexibility and processing. Paraffin base rubber plasticizers, sourced from paraffinic petrochemicals, provide excellent compatibility and low volatility. Cycloalkyl base rubber plasticizers, derived from cycloalkanes, offer superior thermal stability and performance in high-temperature applications. These plasticizers play crucial roles in improving the mechanical and physical characteristics of rubber products, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
