<p><ul><li>Ion-Assisted Deposition (IAD)</li><li>Electron Beam Evaporation</li><li>Sputtering</li><li>Vacuum Deposition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lens coating market encompasses various deposition technologies for enhancing optical performance. Ion-Assisted Deposition (IAD) combines physical and chemical processes to improve film adherence and durability. Electron Beam Evaporation uses high-energy electrons to vaporize materials, creating thin, uniform coatings ideal for high-performance optics. Sputtering involves ejecting particles from a target material to deposit films with precise control over thickness and composition. Vacuum Deposition operates in a controlled environment, allowing for the deposition of films while minimizing contamination, ensuring high-quality optical coatings.</p></p>
