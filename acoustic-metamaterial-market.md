<p><ul><li>Resonant Membrane</li><li>Periodic Resonators</li><li>Sonic Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acoustic metamaterial market can be categorized into three main types: resonant membranes, periodic resonators, and sonic crystals. Resonant membranes utilize thin materials that exhibit unique sound absorption properties through resonant behavior. Periodic resonators consist of structured elements arranged in patterns to manipulate sound waves, enhancing specific frequencies. Sonic crystals are composed of periodic structures that scatter sound waves, creating band gaps, which can effectively control sound propagation. These types are essential for improving noise control and acoustic performance in various applications.</p></p>
