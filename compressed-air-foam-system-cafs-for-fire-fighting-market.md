<p><p>A Compressed Air Foam System (CAFS) for firefighting utilizes a mixture of water, foam concentrate, and compressed air to produce foam that suppresses fires effectively. The market is segmented into three types based on foam expansion: Low-expansion foam systems create a dense foam that clings to surfaces, making it ideal for structural fires. Medium-expansion foam systems provide a balance of coverage and penetration, while high-expansion foam systems generate large volumes of airy foam, suitable for confined spaces and rapidly extinguishing fires.</p></p>
